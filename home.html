About

Piñatas Hola Soy Alondra is a small business created and run by a Latina Woman. This business started about 4 years ago when Alondra made a piñata for her son’s birthday party. Her friend was astonished by the uniqueness and craftsmanship of the piñata that without a doubt she encouraged her to sell the piñatas.

Alondra for the most part makes piñatas, which are made out of cardboard and colorful tissue paper. She also makes other decorations for parties such as picture frames, table decorations and more.
